### ✨ Why choose HabitForge AI?
* **🤖 AI Goal Breakdown:** Don't know where to start? Just type your big goal, and the AI inside will instantly break it down into simple, manageable daily habits.
* **🌱 Healthy Lifestyle:** Easily build and track routines that boost your physical and mental well-being.
* **🚀 Lightweight & Fast:** Highly optimized! It takes up very little space on your phone and won't drain your battery.
* **📱 Always at Hand:** A clean, minimalist interface keeps your focus exactly on what matters most.

### 📥 How to Install on Android
1. Click on the **`app-release.apk`** file in the list above.
2. Click the **Download raw file** button (the icon with a downward arrow on the right side).
3. Open the downloaded file on your Android phone and click Install. 
*(Note: Your phone might ask for permission to "Install from unknown sources" since it's downloaded directly from GitHub. It is completely safe!)*
